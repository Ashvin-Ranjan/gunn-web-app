Did your power go out today? Fortunately, UGWA's didn't! Here are some extra features to start off the school year.

  • There's now an option to show notifications a given time before a class starts or ends. I don't think iOS Safari supports notifications, though.
  • You can now swipe between days; there's an option to turn this off if needed (on by default).
  • You can click on a day in the week overview to jump to it.
  • The Gunn Together period on Wednesday now has a badge to avoid confusion over why the first week has two adjacent period 5's.
